1. clipPath — Star-shaped Clip

A <clipPath> defines a region. Any element with clip-path="url(#starClip)" is only visible where the star overlaps it. Try changing the star's points!

2. Mask — Text Cut Out of a Gradient

A <mask> uses white for "show" and black for "hide". The white text reveals the rainbow gradient behind it. The black rectangle hides everything else.

3. Pattern — Dot Swirl

A <pattern> is a reusable tile. We draw a tiny square (20×20) with a dot in it, then fill a large circle with url(#dotPattern). Also see the stripes variant below.
